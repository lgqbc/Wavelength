About Session Notes
Note taking for audio purposes
  • Whether you're working on a track and want to brainstorm, or want to take notes while studying references, this tool makes it convenient.
  • Choose an audio file from your PC, use file-less mode for an external audio source, or record straight from your mic.
  • Creating a new note places it at that spot in the timeline.
  • In live mode, a new note is created as soon as you begin typing. Hit enter when done, and the tool is ready for you to start typing again.
  • Export your notes with timestamps as .txt or .md text files.
